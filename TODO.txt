General:

What is a proper request?
Different conditions that would result in these responses: 200 OK, 400 Bad Request, 403 Forbidden,
404 Not Found, 500 Internal Service Error, and 501 Not Implemented


Sockets:
1. Create a socket
2. Bind socket to address
3. Listen on the address
4. Block on Accept until the connection is made
5. Read on the connected socket
6. Figure out how to respond
7. Write back on the connected socket
8. Close the connection
9. Go back to blocking on accept
